Pork Barbeque

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs pork shoulder
  • 3/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup calamansi/lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup banana ketchup
  • 4 tbsp dark brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups lemon/lime soda

Steps

  1. Place pork slices in large bowl.
  2. Add all marinade ingredients and mix well. Cover bowl and marinate for at least 3 hours (preferably overnight).
  3. Skewer the pork with bamboo skewers.
  4. Grill the barbecue 3-5 min per side until cooked. Baste the barbecue when flipping. The basting sauce is the same as the marinade minus the soda.
  5. Serve with spicy vinegar
